Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

We have proudly been a member of the CPD Standards Office since 2023. Having successfully passed the rigorous assessment process, we are now an accredited CPD provider. This prestigious accreditation highlights our commitment to delivering high-quality CPD-accredited training and allows us to issue formal CPD certificates upon course completion. This ensures that delegates meet mandatory and reflective CPD requirements with ease.

Make an Appointment

Why CPD

Accreditation through the CPD Standards Office means that any professional, from any industry, can claim a CPD certificate from an approved CPD provider. It is a streamlined process, and for training providers with a rich and diverse set of delegates, it saves time and simplifies registering with multiple institutes and regulators.

By completing CPD-accredited learning activities, delegates can be confident that they are consuming high-quality, innovative, and informative content that is immediately applicable in the workplace. Whether professionals have a mandatory CPD record to maintain or not, undertaking and recording formal CPD is a great way to demonstrate a commitment to staying up to date.

Benefits of CPD

  • Keeping skills and knowledge up to date
  • Career progression
  • A professional sense of direction
  • Professional standards of qualifications and registration are maintained
  • Confidence and credibility are built and enhanced
