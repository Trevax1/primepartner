Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

We have proudly been a member of the CPD Standards Office since 2023. Having successfully passed the rigorous assessment process, we are now an accredited CPD provider. This prestigious accreditation highlights our commitment to delivering high-quality CPD-accredited training and allows us to issue formal CPD certificates upon course completion. This ensures that delegates meet mandatory and reflective CPD requirements with ease.

Why CPD?

Accreditation through the CPD Standards Office provides significant benefits to professionals across all industries. With our CPD accreditation, you can confidently claim CPD certificates from a trusted provider, streamlining your professional development process. For training providers with diverse delegate needs, this accreditation saves time and reduces the complexity of dealing with multiple institutes and regulators.

By engaging in CPDSO-accredited learning activities, you ensure that the content is not only high-quality but also immediately applicable in the workplace. Whether maintaining a mandatory CPD record or simply demonstrating a commitment to professional growth, our formal CPD offerings are designed to support your ongoing development effectively.

  • Keeping skills and knowledge up to date
  • Supporting career progression
  • Providing a professional sense of direction
  • Maintaining professional standards of qualifications and registrations
  • Enhancing confidence and credibility
