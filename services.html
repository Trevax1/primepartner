Online health and Social Care Courses

We are a London based care training provider offering training courses for both individuals and organizations working in the care industry.

Care Quality Commission (CQC) registration

We help new care providers with CQC Registration using our experience in the industry which we have gained over the years.

Without the necessary knowledge & guidance, the CQC registration process can be a headache and an obstacle for many start-ups and expanding businesses as in most cases they need to start their business operations as soon as possible.

Who this service is for?

  • Start-ups
  • Expanding businesses
