Welcome To Prime Partnership & Training

We are a UK - London based Care Training provider offering training courses for individuals and organizations working in the care industry.

We also help care startups and Expanding care businesses with Care Quality Commission (CQC) registration, a mandatory prerequisite for all regulated activities.

One of the most stressful aspects of running a social care company is dealing with CQC Inspections. Our consultants have the up-to-date knowledge and experience to support you and your staff and carry out CQC Pre-Inspection Audits. Thus, ensuring your services meet the CQC’s fundamental standards.
